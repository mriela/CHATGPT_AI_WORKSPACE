Purpose:

## Poster Purpose
- Announce the VRChat音楽イベント clearly.
- Communicate the event mood, music style, and venue atmosphere.
- Provide visual material that can also be reused inside the Unity event venue.

## Required Information
- Event name: VRChat音楽イベント
- Date and time
- Venue or world name
- Performer, DJ, or organizer names
- Main music genre or theme
- Join method or access notes
- Any sponsor, credit, or logo requirements

## Design Ideas
- Use a strong title area that is readable in both poster and in-world display sizes.
- Use lighting, stage, sound wave, or neon-inspired visual motifs.
- Keep important text high contrast for VRChat viewing.
- Prepare one main poster direction and one simpler in-world display variant.
