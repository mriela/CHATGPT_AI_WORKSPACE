# Poster Design Variations

Purpose:
- Organize three poster design directions for the VRChat music event.
- Help MRIELA prepare poster materials early and help SPALITUDA match them with the Unity venue.

## 1. 幻想的・星空系

### Concept
- A dreamy music event floating under a wide night sky.
- Emphasize calm immersion, mystery, and a special one-night atmosphere.

### Color Palette
- Deep navy
- Star white
- Pale blue
- Soft violet
- Small accents of gold or cyan

### Font Direction
- Elegant serif or thin sans-serif for the event title.
- Use clean, readable sans-serif for date, venue, and performer information.
- Keep letter spacing calm and spacious.

### Background Motifs
- Star field
- Moonlight
- Constellation lines
- Floating particles
- Mist or soft clouds

### Main Visual
- A small stage or silhouette under a large sky.
- A glowing microphone, piano, or DJ booth surrounded by stars.
- Optional avatar silhouettes looking toward the stage.

### Unity Venue Matching Points
- Match poster colors with skybox, ambient light, and particle effects.
- Use star, moon, or constellation motifs in the venue decorations.
- Place poster boards near calm viewing areas where text remains readable.

### Materials MRIELA Can Prepare Early
- Color palette reference
- Poster layout rough
- Starry background image direction
- Title typography samples
- Short announcement copy with a dreamy tone

## 2. カフェライブ・温かい雰囲気系

### Concept
- A friendly live event in a cozy VRChat cafe.
- Emphasize warmth, closeness, acoustic performance, and relaxed conversation.

### Color Palette
- Warm cream
- Coffee brown
- Amber light
- Muted red or orange
- Soft green accents

### Font Direction
- Rounded sans-serif or friendly handwritten-style title font.
- Use simple sans-serif for important event details.
- Keep the overall look approachable and easy to read.

### Background Motifs
- Cafe table
- Warm lamps
- Wood textures
- Coffee cups
- Small indoor stage

### Main Visual
- Performer area inside a cafe.
- Acoustic instruments, microphone stand, or small speaker setup.
- Warm window light or hanging lamps behind the event title.

### Unity Venue Matching Points
- Match poster warmth with venue lighting, wood materials, and props.
- Use poster colors for signage, menu boards, or stage decorations.
- Keep the poster placement near entrance, counter, or stage path.

### Materials MRIELA Can Prepare Early
- Cozy copy variations
- Cafe-style layout rough
- Warm color palette
- Prop and background reference list
- Simple poster draft for entrance signage

## 3. 近未来・クラブイベント系

### Concept
- A high-energy VRChat club event with a futuristic digital atmosphere.
- Emphasize rhythm, motion, neon, and immersive crowd energy.

### Color Palette
- Black or dark charcoal
- Neon cyan
- Magenta
- Electric purple
- White highlights

### Font Direction
- Bold geometric sans-serif for the event title.
- Condensed or techno-style display font for accent text.
- Keep schedule and access details in a clear sans-serif for readability.

### Background Motifs
- Neon grid
- Laser lights
- Equalizer waves
- Holographic panels
- Digital glitch textures

### Main Visual
- DJ booth or stage with strong backlight.
- Abstract sound waves or holographic event logo.
- Crowd silhouettes, neon frames, or floating UI panels.

### Unity Venue Matching Points
- Match poster neon colors with club lighting and emissive materials.
- Reuse equalizer, grid, or hologram motifs in Unity props.
- Test poster brightness so it is visible without overexposure in dark areas.

### Materials MRIELA Can Prepare Early
- Neon color palette
- Typographic title treatments
- Abstract background direction
- Short energetic announcement copy
- In-world sign variant with fewer details
